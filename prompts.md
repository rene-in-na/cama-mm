# Dig Minigame Art — Nano Banana Prompts

All images should have a dark underground fantasy RPG aesthetic. Think dungeon crawler meets Dota 2 bestiary.

---

## Boss Encounter Portraits (512x288, landscape)

These appear when a player first reaches a boss boundary. The boss should be imposing, center-frame, with a small miner figure approaching from the left. Dramatic torch/ambient lighting.

### Grothak the Unbreakable (Depth 25 — Dirt Layer)
```
A massive stone golem guardian blocking a narrow dirt tunnel underground, glowing amber eyes, crude rocky armor, arms crossed defiantly, a tiny miner with a pickaxe approaching from the left side, torchlight casting warm shadows on brown earthen walls, roots hanging from the ceiling, fantasy RPG boss encounter, dark earthy brown palette, digital painting, dramatic composition
```

### Crystalia the Refracted (Depth 50 — Crystal Layer)
```
A tall crystalline humanoid entity made of refracting prisms and sharp geometric crystal shards, standing in a cave of teal and cyan crystal formations, light splitting into rainbow refractions, perfectly symmetrical composition, a small miner with a pickaxe visible at the left edge, underground crystal cavern with glowing geodes, fantasy RPG boss, digital painting, cool cyan palette
```

### Magmus Rex (Depth 75 — Magma Layer)
```
A colossal fire elemental king with a molten crown, sitting on a jagged volcanic rock throne, rivers of glowing lava flowing around the base, looking imperious and slightly annoyed, embers and sparks floating upward, a tiny miner silhouetted against the lava glow at the left, underground magma chamber, deep red and orange palette with golden highlights, fantasy RPG boss, digital painting
```

### The Void Warden (Depth 100 — Abyss Layer)
```
An amorphous entity of pure swirling darkness with a single glowing violet V-shaped sigil for a face, floating in an abyssal underground void, tendrils of shadow extending outward, deep purple and black palette, oppressive atmosphere of existential dread, a small miner holding a faint lantern at the left barely illuminating the darkness, fantasy RPG boss, digital painting
```

### Sporeling Sovereign (Depth 150 — Fungal Depths)

A massive collective mushroom hivemind entity, thousands of bioluminescent mushrooms of varying sizes forming a towering vaguely humanoid shape, mycelium tendrils spreading across the cave floor and walls, eerie green and cyan bioluminescent glow, spore particles floating in the air, a small miner dwarfed at the left side, underground fungal cavern, fantasy RPG, digital painting

### Chronofrost (Depth 200 — Frozen Core)

A frozen time entity made of shattered ice crystals suspended in mid-explosion, clock hands and frozen water droplets floating motionless around it, crystalline blue ice cavern with massive icicles, time distortion visual effects like motion blur frozen in place, frost particles suspended in air, a small miner approaching from the left, deep blue palette, fantasy RPG boss, digital painting

## File Naming Convention

Place generated images in:
```
assets/dig/bosses/{slug}_{scene}.png
assets/dig/layers/{slug}.png
```

### Boss slugs:
| Depth | Slug |
|-------|------|
| 25 | `grothak` |
| 50 | `crystalia` |
| 75 | `magmus` |
| 100 | `void_warden` |
| 150 | `sporeling` |
| 200 | `chronofrost` |
| 275 | `nameless` |

### Scene types: `encounter`, `victory`, `defeat`

### Layer slugs: `dirt`, `stone`, `crystal`, `magma`, `abyss`, `fungal_depths`, `frozen_core`, `the_hollow`

### Examples:
```
assets/dig/bosses/grothak_encounter.png
assets/dig/bosses/grothak_victory.png
assets/dig/bosses/grothak_defeat.png
assets/dig/bosses/crystalia_encounter.png
assets/dig/layers/dirt.png
assets/dig/layers/crystal.png
```

### Image Dimensions:
- Boss scenes: **512x288** (16:9 landscape, fits Discord embed main image)
- Layer thumbnails: **128x128** (square, used as Discord embed thumbnail)
- Item icons: **128x128** (square, used as Discord embed thumbnail)
- Pickaxe icons: **128x128** (square, used as Discord embed thumbnail)
